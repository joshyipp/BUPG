About Us

At a university that is extremely vigilant of the inclusivity, enthusiasm, and energy it can breed, unifying the student body is a spirited goal of BU CAB. More importantly, we emphasize affordability, safety, and coordination of our events. This year, BU will host our very own TerrierFest. From artist concerts, DJ stages, and student performances, we hope that Terrierfest can provide the best entertainment for all students. Given the unique nature of this event and its ability to bring together thousands of students, we work hard to ensure everyone is happy to be at BU. We are constantly evolving and improving to offer high quality events exclusive to the BU community.
